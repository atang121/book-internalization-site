⚠️ Common Visual Issues to Check

❌ Broken Layout Issues
Check for these problems:
  • Sidebar overlapping content area
  • Text overflowing containers
  • Icons misaligned with labels
  • Active state not visible (border or background missing)
  • Multiple items active at once
  • Sidebar not sticky (disappears when scrolling)
⚠️ Typography Issues
Font problems to watch for:
  • Labels too small or too large (should be 0.95rem ≈ 15px)
  • Font not loading (should be PingFang SC for Chinese)
  • Letter spacing too tight or too loose
  • Unreadable text colors (too dim)
🎨 Color & Visual Issues
Visual problems:
  • Gold accent not showing (#d4a574 should be visible)
  • Gradient background not subtle enough or too subtle
  • Icons not changing opacity between active/inactive
  • No grayscale effect on inactive icons
  • Left border not 3px or wrong color
🖱️ Interaction Issues
Functional problems:
  • Sidebar items not clickable
  • Scroll tracking not updating active state
  • No smooth scrolling when clicking sidebar
  • Active state not updating as you scroll
  • Hover effects not working
